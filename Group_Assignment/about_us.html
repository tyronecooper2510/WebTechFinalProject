Who We Are

INFOServ is an IT Consulting & Support
Services company. We help our clients
achieve their business objectives by
leveraging their IT resources to provide
them with stable, secure and productive
computing environments.

Our Mission

INFOServ is a Jamaican company that
delivers world-class IT services through
empowered and engaged team members
who provide an incredibly good customer
experience. We provide a positive and
honest environment which embraces change.
INFOServ will impact the markets in which
we operate by consistently raising the bar for
the industry through continuous improvement
and innovation.
