Our Mission

Info Serv is a Jamaican company that delivers world-class IT services through empowered and engaged team members who provide an incredibly good customer experience. We provide a positive and honest environment which embraces change. Info Serv will impact the markets in which we operate by consistently raising the bar for the industry through continuous improvement and innovation.

Our History

The brainchild of several experts in the areas of server management, network management, IT security, applications development and support, Info Serve was founded by Rolan Robinson, a former Director of The National Environment and Planning Agency (NEPA), in collaboration with other senior IT professionals.
