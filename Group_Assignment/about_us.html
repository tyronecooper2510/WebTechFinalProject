Our Story

Established in late 2017, Info Serv set out on a mission to help small & medium-sized businesses understand and make efficient use of their Information Technology (IT) resources. Since then we’ve assisted numerous local and regional businesses achieve their objectives by leveraging their IT resources to provide them with stable, secure and productive computing environments.

We currently offer our customers IT consulting services, as well as IT system support and maintenance services – and we’d be happy to help you too!
